Run For Her — Every Step for Strength

As part of its Silver Jubilee, New Horizon College of Engineering presents New Horizon Unity Run 2026. Bangalore’s largest student-driven marathon on 8th March, International Women’s Day. Open to all ages and professionals with 3K, 5K, and 10K categories, it celebrates fitness, community, and purpose.

Who can Participate

  • A thoughtfully inclusive run for every age group who are fit for the run, from age 6 can pace in and support, For Her.
  • Working professionals, students, and families are welcome.
