Run For Her — Every Step for Strength

As part of the Silver Jubilee Year Celebrations, New Horizon College of Engineering presents New Horizon Unity Run 2026. Taking place on International Women’s Day, the run is open to all ages and professionals with 3K, 5K, and 10K categories to celebrate fitness, community, and purpose.
